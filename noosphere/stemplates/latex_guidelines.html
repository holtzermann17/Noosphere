LaTeX Submission Guidelines

A standard template will be applied to your submission, giving you the AMS math package. All you need to do is give us everything between \begin{document} and \end{document}. The template looks like:

"$math" is replaced with your submission.

"$packages" is currently replaced \usepackage{html} if html commands (needed for reference linking) are within your entry.

"$preamble" is where the preamble you create is inserted.

If you have any ideas for improvement regarding math templates, please give us some feedback.
