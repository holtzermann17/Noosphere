Feedback

There are plenty of ways to reach the Team:

  • You can use the " Comments" Forum, in the "Forums" section, and post a message.

  • You can send us an e-mail message at . Please do not send questions of a mathematical nature here! Use the forums instead. The administrator(s) do not have the time, and more often than not, the ability to answer your math questions.

  • You can speak with us real-time on IRC. Currently we are set up on a little-known network called Endernet, in #planetmath. You can use pdox.endernet.org, br.endernet.org, dominia.endernet.org, or just irc.endernet.org. Just ask your question and someone should be able to help you out.

Please use the above for comments and criticism about itself. We are especially interested in hearing about ideas for improving and making it more usable to you and your community.

However... before you contact us, heed the following:

  • If you have found an error in an entry, please make an account and file a correction (by going to the entry and clicking "correct"). The development team does not write the encyclopedia entries!

  • If you have a bug to report, please use our bugzilla system to enter it in. This makes it much more likely that the bug will eventually get fixed.

  • If you have a question about the nature of , or how to use it, please first read our FAQ and perhaps browse the rest of our documentation.

  • We reiterate: do not contact the admin(s) with mathematics questions! Please utilize the community by posting a message to the entry of concern, posting a correction, or posting to the general forums. Making your discourse public also allows others to benefit.
Thanks! We hope to hear from you soon.
